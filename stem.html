📜 OUR HISTORY

The Dummy 13 started as an open-source project. SQUAD 3D evolved it by engineering a Hybrid Build: Transparent Blue PETG skeletons for strength, and Carbon Fiber (CF-PLA) armor for a "Diamond" finish.

🧪 MATERIAL SCIENCE

PETG: Used for joints. It's flexible so it doesn't snap during dance moves.

CF-PLA: Used for armor. Infused with carbon fibers to be 3x stiffer than plastic.
